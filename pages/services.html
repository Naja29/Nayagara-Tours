SERVICE 04

Visa Assistance

Most visitors to Sri Lanka require an Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) obtained online before arrival. The process, while straightforward, can be confusing — especially around document requirements, photo specifications, and processing times. Our team guides you through every step, reviews your application before submission, and helps resolve any issues quickly so your travel paperwork is always in order.

  • Step-by-step ETA application guidance
  • Document checklist & pre-submission review
  • Visa-on-arrival assistance at Colombo airport
  • Visa extension support for long-stay travelers
  • Multiple-entry & business visa advisory
  • Emergency visa support available 24/7
