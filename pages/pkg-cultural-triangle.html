Tour Highlights

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Climb the iconic 5th-century rock citadel, one of Sri Lanka's most dramatic UNESCO sites, and admire ancient frescoes along the way.

Dambulla Cave Temples

Explore five stunning cave sanctuaries filled with over 150 golden Buddha statues and ancient murals covering 2,100 sq meters.

Polonnaruwa Ruins

Cycle through the medieval royal capital's vast archaeological park — moonstone carvings, colossal Buddhas, and palace ruins await.

Temple of the Tooth

Witness the sacred evening Puja ceremony at Kandy's Temple of the Tooth Relic — Sri Lanka's holiest Buddhist shrine.

Anuradhapura Sacred City

Discover the world's oldest documented tree — the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi — and explore towering ancient dagobas over 2,000 years old.

Kandyan Cultural Show

Enjoy a vibrant Kandyan cultural performance featuring traditional dance, fire-walkers, and the electrifying Ves dance of the temple dancers.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

01
Arrive Colombo → Sigiriya Airport pickup · Scenic drive · Village welcome dinner
  • Welcome by your Nayagara Tours guide at Bandaranaike International Airport
  • Comfortable A/C transfer (approx. 3.5 hrs) north to Sigiriya passing through Sri Lanka's lush countryside
  • Check-in at your boutique hotel; freshen up and relax
  • Evening guided walk through a traditional Sigiriya village — interact with local families, see paddy fields, and enjoy a home-cooked dinner
Dinner Included
02
Sigiriya Rock & Pidurangala UNESCO Fortress · Ancient frescoes · Panoramic views
  • Early morning climb of Sigiriya Rock Fortress (sunrise optional) — see the famous frescoes (Sigiri Apsaras), the Mirror Wall with 8th-century graffiti, and enjoy 360° views from the summit palace
  • Return to hotel for breakfast and rest during the midday heat
  • Afternoon: Climb Pidurangala Rock for a spectacular sunset view directly opposite Sigiriya — a favourite photography spot
  • Evening at leisure; optional hot-air balloon flight available (extra cost)
Breakfast Included
03
Dambulla Caves & Polonnaruwa Golden Buddhas · Royal ruins · Bicycle tour
  • Morning visit to the Dambulla Royal Cave Temple — a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring 5 caves, 153 Buddha statues, and magnificent ceiling frescoes
  • Drive east to Polonnaruwa — Sri Lanka's medieval capital (11th–13th century)
  • Explore Polonnaruwa Archeological Park by bicycle (bicycles provided) — Royal Palace, Gal Vihara rock temple with four colossal Buddha figures, Lotus Pond, and the Lankatilaka image house
  • Evening transfer to your hotel in the Polonnaruwa area
Breakfast Included
04
Anuradhapura Sacred City World's oldest tree · Ancient dagobas · Sri Maha Bodhi
  • Full day in Anuradhapura — Sri Lanka's first ancient capital and sacred city, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Visit the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi — a fig tree grown from a cutting of the original Bodhi tree under which the Buddha attained enlightenment (2,300+ years old)
  • Explore Ruwanwelisaya Dagoba, Thuparamaya (first dagoba in Sri Lanka), Jetavanaramaya (once among the world's tallest structures), and the Royal Palace ruins
  • Afternoon drive south to Kandy (approx. 3.5 hrs); check-in and rest
Breakfast Included
05
Kandy — Cultural Capital Temple of the Tooth · Botanical Gardens · Cultural Show
  • Morning visit to the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa) — witness the morning Puja ceremony, one of the most sacred rituals in Sri Lanka
  • Explore the Royal Botanical Gardens of Peradeniya — 147 acres of tropical plants, giant bamboo, orchid house, and a famous collection of royal palms
  • Visit a spice garden and learn about Sri Lanka's famous cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves
  • Evening: Kandyan Cultural Show with traditional drumming, fire-walking, and Ves dance performance
  • Evening Puja ceremony at the Temple of the Tooth (optional — highly recommended)
Breakfast Included
06
Kandy → Colombo — Departure Scenic drive · Last-minute shopping · Airport drop
  • Relaxed breakfast at your hotel
  • Optional morning visit to a batik or handloom silk workshop — Sri Lanka's famous textile art tradition
  • Drive to Colombo (approx. 3 hrs) — optional stop at Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage to see elephants bathing in the river
  • Drop-off at Bandaranaike International Airport for your onward flight. Safe travels!
Breakfast Included
